Research on the Influence of Social Media upon the Stock Market

Data mining • NLP sentiment • ML/DL • Econometrics

If the image file is not available, remove this tag or replace with your own.

We analyze and predict stock market dynamics using large-scale social media data via web crawling, text mining, sentiment modeling, and machine learning. Unstructured posts are transformed into an emotion index, and the predictive power of linear models and deep neural networks is evaluated empirically.

Methodologies

  • Web crawling & large-scale text processing
  • NLP sentiment analysis & index construction
  • ML classification & LSTM-based forecasting
  • Econometric modeling (time series)

Key Findings

  • ML sentiment reaches ~88% accuracy
  • Sentiment negatively correlates with volatility
  • LSTM outperforms linear regression for prediction
  • Short-term predictive signal from social sentiment
